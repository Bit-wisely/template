About Us

We are a student-led robotics community at UCE, fostering automation, electronics, and design thinking.

Our club provides a creative space where hardware engineers, programmers, and designers collaborate on real-world projects. From autonomous drone navigation to industrial automation prototypes, we push the limits of technology.
